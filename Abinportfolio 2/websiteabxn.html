About Me

I'm a second-year user experience student and cinematographer, passionate about blending film and design to create visually compelling stories. My expertise lies in crafting cinematic, film-style short videos for Instagram and directing trailer shoots for college events, where I handle cinematography, direction, and editing. Through my work, I strive to capture emotion, atmosphere, and narrative depth, turning everyday moments into captivating visual experiences.

 

 

You can often find me exploring new storytelling techniques, experimenting with unique compositions, or refining my editing style to push creative boundaries. Whether behind the camera or in post-production, I’m driven by a desire to bring ideas to life in fresh and engaging ways. My goal is to innovate, challenge conventions, and craft visuals that leave a lasting impact.
