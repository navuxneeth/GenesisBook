Gabriela Silva
This portrait features a young woman with striking green eyes and dark curly hair adorned with dried flowers and twigs. She wears a thoughtful expression, her lips slightly parted and painted deep red, while her fingers gently touch her mouth. The soft lighting accentuates the texture of her skin, highlighting freckles and subtle shadows.
