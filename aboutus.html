Who we are

We are second-year UX design students at MIT Institute of Design, Pune, with a shared passion for designing meaningful experiences. Our diverse skills span UX research, AI-driven innovation, graphic design, and videography, shaping how users interact with digital interfaces.

Our vision

To push the boundaries of design by merging creativity with technology, crafting experiences that are functional, engaging, and future-ready. We strive to create intuitive digital interactions that not only enhance user engagement but also drive meaningful change.
